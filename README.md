### **Entry**

Some individuals report that falling asleep on a bus late at night may result in arrival at this level.

Upon waking, the individual is no longer inside the bus.

Instead, they are standing outside a small rural house.

The bus is gone.

No road. No driver. No sound.

Only a faint porch light, and a garden ahead.

---

### **Description**

Level ??? appears as an isolated village house surrounded by an overgrown garden.

Lighting is extremely low. In some cases, a distant streetlamp may be visible, though it provides little visibility.

The environment is unnaturally silent.

Once an individual steps into the garden, the level begins to change.

The area operates in two states: the **Spying Zone** and the **Danger Zone**.

---

### **Spying Zone**

At first, nothing attacks.

However, nearly all individuals report the same feeling:

They are being watched.

Thin, web-like formations can be seen attached to walls, poles, ceilings, and garden structures. At a distance, they appear to be ordinary spider webs.

This is misleading.

On closer inspection, the strands appear unnatural—too straight, too structured, and placed in ways that do not match normal web patterns.

Some individuals report seeing shapes inside the webbing.

Not clear shapes.

Something blended into it.

A body.

But it never fully reveals itself in this state.

This is believed to be an entity known as **“K”;_ Spy**.

It remains completely still, using the web-like structure as camouflage.

Occasionally, slight movement may be perceived, though it is unclear whether this is real or psychological.

The longer an individual remains in this zone, the stronger the feeling of being watched becomes.

Eventually, the level changes.

---

### **Danger Zone**

The transition is not clearly defined.

It may be triggered by time, awareness, or fear.

When it begins:

* The darkness deepens
* The environment feels distorted
* Distances seem inconsistent

And the truth about the “webs” becomes clear.

They are not webs.

They are part of the entity.

**“K”;_ Spy** reveals its full form:

* A thin, stretched body integrated into web-like strands
* Long, unnatural limb extensions spread across surfaces
* A structure that appears both attached and alive
* No clear face, though some report eye-like shapes within the strands

It does not leave its location.

It does not chase.

Instead, it expands within its area, revealing more of itself the closer you get.

What appeared small in the Spying Zone may actually be much larger.

By the time its full shape is understood—

escape is unlikely.

Contact results in immediate disappearance.

No sound. No trace.

---

### **The Bus Stop**

If an individual manages to exit the garden, they may find a bus stop.

The area remains silent.

After an unknown amount of time, a bus may arrive.Bi 

---

### **Normal Bus**

A standard bus may appear.

Boarding it may lead to:

* Return to reality
* Transition to another Backrooms level

Results are inconsistent.

---

### **The Red Bus**

In rare cases, a **Red Bus** arrives instead.

It appears dark red, sometimes almost black.

It makes no sound.

There is no visible driver.

The doors open on their own.

---

### **Inside the Red Bus**

The interior is dim and quiet.

Reported anomalies include:

* The space feeling larger than it should be
* Windows that do not show the outside
* Reflections that move incorrectly
* The constant feeling that something else is present

No entity is clearly seen.

But the feeling does not go away.

---

### **Primary Effect**

The Red Bus does not allow escape.

It returns the individual to the same house.

But the level is no longer the same.

---

### **Cognitive Distortion**

After exiting the Red Bus, the level becomes influenced by the individual’s mind.

This is the true danger.

Reports suggest:

> Thoughts begin to affect reality.

* Thinking something is watching you may cause movement to appear
* Expecting an entity may result in one forming
* Fear increases the intensity of all anomalies

At this stage, it becomes unclear what is real.

---

### **Distorted Entities**

After Red Bus exposure:

* New entities may appear
* Some resemble unnatural or “costumed” figures
* Their behavior may feel artificial or staged

It is unknown whether these are:

* New entities
* Altered versions of **“K”;_ Spy**
* Or direct creations of the individual’s mind

Despite this uncertainty, contact still results in disappearance.

---

### **Mental Escalation**

Each use of the Red Bus appears to:

* Increase instability
* Strengthen thought-based manifestations
* Reduce the ability to distinguish reality

Individuals may begin to experience:

* Visuals that match their fears
* Sounds that occur immediately after being imagined
* The sensation of creating their own threats

---

### **Theory (Unconfirmed)**

Some believe:

The first visit is the real level.

Every return after the Red Bus is shaped by the mind.

This may explain why no two experiences are identical.

---

### **Warnings**

* Do not remain in the garden for too long
* Do not approach web-like formations
* Do not focus on the feeling of being watched

And most importantly:

> Do not trust your thoughts after entering the Red Bus.

---

### **Exits**

* Boarding a normal bus at the bus stop
* Unknown exits may exist

Repeated entry into the Red Bus may result in permanent entrapment.

---

### **Final Note**

Some survivors claim:

> The level does not create the horror.

> You do.
